Bruce Lee

Description

The mighty Bruce Lee, and a template showcasing his rivalry with the equally well known Chuck Norris. Who wouldnt want to make such a display to showcase their pick between the two, as seen here someone prefers Bruce, a welcome choice.

Recipe

  • Bruce Lee image
  • Idea of what text to add to image
  • Image editor such as paint.net or free online alternative
  • Understanding and respect for the power of Bruce Lee

Steps

  1. Open image editor
  2. Add Bruce Lee image to editor
  3. Place text over Bruce Lee image
  4. Prosper in sight of the Mighty Bruce Lee and your text surrounding him
