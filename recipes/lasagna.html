Lasagna

Description

A high in protein and carbs meal that can be preped within 30 minutes. A suitable choice for those who partake in physcial activity and wish to fuel their muscle growth adequately.

  • Italian sausage and lean ground beef.
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Can of crushed Tomatos, two cans of tomato sauce, two cans of tomato paste.
  • Two tablespoons of sugar
  • Fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, Italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper.
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • One Egg

Steps

  1. Make meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. Cake the ricotta mixture
  4. Cayer the Lasagna according to the recipe instructions
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving
